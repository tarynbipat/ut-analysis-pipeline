# ut-contradiction-reconciler Skill

This agent does **not** treat contradictions as errors. It treats them as useful research signal.

## What it does

- Preserves tensions instead of flattening them into false consensus
- Explains why contradictions may exist
- Produces reconciled interpretations rather than averaging disagreements away
- Surfaces design implications and follow-up research questions

## How it explains tensions

The reconciler looks for plausible explanation factors such as:

- **Segment** differences
- **Experience level**
- **Task context**
- **Product expectation**
- **Evidence limits**

It can also acknowledge when other unknown factors may be involved.

## Why contradictions happen

Contradictions may reflect meaningful variation in:

- user segment
- experience level
- task context
- product expectations
- limits in the available evidence

These are treated as valuable signals about where user experience differs, not as mistakes to discard.

## Output philosophy

Instead of saying users had "mixed feelings" and averaging disagreements away, this skill:

1. describes the tension clearly
2. suggests why the contradiction exists
3. preserves both sides in a reconciled interpretation
4. recommends research or design actions that account for both perspectives
